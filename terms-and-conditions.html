These Terms govern your access to and use of the FikaConnect platform (website, app and services). By using FikaConnect, you agree to these Terms and the laws of the Republic of Uganda.

1) Definitions

  • Platform: FikaConnect website, mobile app, and related tools.
  • User: Any person using the Platform (senders, receivers, transporters).
  • Transporter: Truck owners, drivers, vans, boda-boda riders registered on the Platform.
  • Sender: Any person or entity requesting a delivery service.
  • Receiver: The intended recipient of goods.
  • Goods: Any parcel/package/cargo booked for delivery via the Platform.

2) Eligibility

  • You must be at least 18 years old.
  • Transporters must have valid driver’s licences, roadworthy certificates, and comply with the Traffic and Road Safety Act.
  • All Users must use the Platform for lawful purposes and comply with applicable Ugandan laws and regulations.

3) Services Provided

FikaConnect digitally connects senders with available transporters, provides booking & tracking tools, enables proof-of-delivery (photo/signature/code), and offers collection-point workflows. We are not a transport company; we act as a digital intermediary between parties.

Tip: For high-value consignments, consider adding goods insurance and clearly declaring cargo value before dispatch.

4) Permitted Goods (Uganda Law Compliant)

Subject to packaging, safety, and legal requirements, the following may be transported:

  • Agricultural produce (e.g., maize, beans, fruits, tomatoes, matooke), fish & milk (properly packaged for perishables).
  • Packaged and labelled food items.
  • Manufactured goods, household items, clothing & textiles.
  • Electronics & appliances.
  • Building materials.

Compliance: Uganda National Bureau of Standards (UNBS) labelling/packaging where applicable.

5) Prohibited Goods

The following are not allowed on the Platform:

  • Illegal drugs/narcotics (per the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Control Act).
  • Firearms, explosives, ammunition (per the Firearms Act), and hazardous chemicals without permits.
  • Stolen or counterfeit goods (per the Penal Code Act).
  • Live animals without clearance (Animal Industry Department) and human remains/body parts.
  • Pornographic materials (per the Anti-Pornography Act).

We reserve the right to inspect, reject, or cancel bookings that breach laws or safety requirements.

6) Pricing & Payment

  • Fares consider distance, cargo type, urgency, and vehicle class; Platform commission may apply.
  • Payment methods: Mobile Money, bank transfer, or card (where supported).
  • Payment is due before or upon delivery unless otherwise agreed in writing.

7) Loss, Damage, or Accidents

  • Transporters must reasonably safeguard goods in their possession.
  • FikaConnect is not directly liable for loss/theft/damage unless caused by our gross negligence.
  • In case of accident, the transporter must notify the sender immediately and cooperate with authorities.
  • Compensation (if any) is based on declared value and agreed terms prior to dispatch.
  • Claims must be lodged within 48 hours of delivery or notification of loss.
Recommendation: Insure valuable or sensitive consignments and use robust packaging suited to transit risks.

8) Cancellations & Refunds

  • Senders may cancel before driver assignment without charges.
  • Cancellations after driver assignment may attract a fee to compensate the assigned transporter.
  • Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days.

9) Liability

  • We are not responsible for delays due to force majeure (weather, roadblocks, strikes, etc.).
  • Transporters remain fully responsible for goods in their custody and for road safety compliance.

10) Dispute Resolution

Disputes should be raised via the in-app channel first. If unresolved, they may be referred to mediation or arbitration in Uganda.

11) Data Privacy

We collect and process personal data per the Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019. See our Privacy Policy for details on data collection, lawful basis, retention, and user rights.

12) Account Suspension/Termination

We may suspend or terminate accounts for fraud, repeated violations, unsafe conduct, or abuse of the Platform.
